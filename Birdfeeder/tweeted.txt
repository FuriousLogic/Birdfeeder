Mon, 08/July/2019 09:49PM: Until recently, the EU mandatory regulations for new cars would appear to be a resounding success for CO2 standards. The rate of reduction in official average tailpipe CO2 values of new passenger cars in the EU increased from roughly 1% per year to more than 3% per year after their introduction in 2009. However, two factors mean this success is not all that it appears.
